Taxonomy on this platform comprises of three layers viz.- 

- Vocabulary layer
- Content Layer and 
- Framework Layer. 

It is used by the system to arrange, suggest and search for content from its repository. Each content unit is associated with multiple tag words that are part of the platform's vocabulary. Each vocabulary word can be part of one or multiple frameworks.
